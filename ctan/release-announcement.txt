This release adds an optional DIN-style information block for business
correspondence. The new `infoblock` option places fixed reference and contact
fields in the upper-right header area and works in both the basic and modern
letter styles.

The information-block labels are now localized for German, English, French,
Spanish, Italian, Dutch, and Polish. The package documentation and bundled
CTAN examples have also been refreshed with focused English examples for the
basic and modern layouts.
